Hoofit is a resource for finding safe accesible routes in Seattle for the visually impaired community.

1) HoofIt's main functionality provides the user with the ability to search for the most walkable routes to their selected destination. 2) Hoofit also offers recommended destinations based on the most walkable destinations within a radius of selected by the user.

This project is open source -- Feel free to check out our work!
